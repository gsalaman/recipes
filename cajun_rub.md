# Cajun Rub

Trying this on pork for Tom's red-beans-and-rice.  Note no sugar...will probably use cherry wood for the smoke.

* 2 TSP Salt
* 2 TBSP Celeery salt
* 1/4 c Paprika (regular)
* 2 TBSP Thyme
* 2 TBSP Oregano
* 1/4 c Garlic Powder
* 1/4 c Onion Powder
* 1 1/2 TBSP black pepper
* 1 TBSP cayenne
* 2 TSP red pepper flakes (crushed down if you can)
