# Ravioli

## Take 1
First:  Drain ricotta.  

Dough:  Trying [Clever Carrot's proportions](https://www.theclevercarrot.com/2022/10/beginners-guide-to-fresh-homemade-ravioli/)
They want 300g type 00 flour with 185g eggs.  I'm going a little smaller for take 1:  200g flour and 125g eggs...probably 2 eggs.  They do olive oil to make up the difference.  Do a little kneeding, then wrap in plastic wrap and let rest for 1 hr.  I'm gonna try vacc seal in fridge.

Clever Carrot does food processor to mix all that.  Gonna try it that way.

Sauce:  basically my canneloni homemade, with some lasagna tweaks.
* Heat 2 TBSP olive oil over medium heat
* Mince 1/2 white onion FINE.
* Add that and one spoonful garlic to olive oil …cook over medium until softened (10 min)
* Add anchovies until they’ve just disappeared.  1 oz.
* Add one small can tomato paste, one can crushed tomatoes,  Fill crush tomato can with water and add.
* Add 1/2 c red wine.  Bring to boil, then reduce to simmer.
* Simmer until consistency is correct.
* Add 1 tbsp each parsley, oregano, and basil.
* Add 1/2 TSP salt and 1/2 TSP sugar
* simmer for another 5 min

While sauce is simmering, make cheese filling...basically same as lasagna:
* That drained ricotta.
* 5oz fontina
* 1/4 cup parm reginnio
* 2 eggs
* 1/4 tsp nutmeg
* prolly a smidge salt & pepper?

Assembly:
Want two sheets at either 6 or 7.  Trim them up first!  Keep rest of dough covered as you go.
Use stamp to loosely mark where centers are, use ?1 TBSP? scoops filling.  Cover with other sheet.  Some do light water brush to help seal.  Some do egg wash.  Some do neither.  I'm gonna try first sheet nothing, then water if that doesn't work.
Before you stamp, make sure you've got all the air bubbles out (they say use pinkies).  Place on sheet pan dusted with seminola, and keep covered.  (towel?)

Garnish:  maybe a little extra parsely?


To freeze:  place ravioi on sheet pan with parchment paper, dusted with seminola flour.  Don't bother covering...freeze until solid.  Then xfer to parchemnt lined air-tight container.
