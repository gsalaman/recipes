# Shawarma Sauce

(Okay, it's not really shawarma, as it doesn't have cuke in it, but it's pretty tasty!!!)

Blend everyting:

* 1c greek yogurt
* 1 lime juice
* 2 tbsp tahnin (can leave this out...)
* 6 scoops garlic (go regular rather than glenn scoops)
* packet dill
* 15 mint leaves
* 1/2 tsp salt
